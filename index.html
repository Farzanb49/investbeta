About Us

InvestBeta is a organization developed by the youth for the youth. Traditionally it has been very easy to learn programming or photography from the comfort of your own home, for free. Finance however has a different story. It is almost exclusive to learn in a classroom environment, and trying to self-learn it is a challenge of its own. At InvestBETA, we aim to create a platform where youth can learn about finance in a non-traditional way, while still not skipping out on the key areas.
